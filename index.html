Welcome to

GD GOENKA, AURANGABAD

GD Goenka School, Aurangabad, is a premier educational institution that has been shaping the minds and futures of young learners since its inception. With a strong emphasis on academic excellence, extracurricular activities, and holistic development, the school provides a nurturing environment for students to explore their talents and reach their full potential. Our dedicated team of experienced educators is committed to imparting knowledge through innovative teaching methodologies, fostering critical thinking, and instilling values that shape responsible global citizens. We believe in providing a well-rounded education that not only focuses on academic prowess but also cultivates creativity, physical fitness, and ethical values. At GD Goenka School, we strive to create an atmosphere that celebrates diversity, encourages curiosity, and inspires students to become lifelong learners, prepared to navigate the challenges of the 21st century with confidence and resilience.

From the Principal’s Desk

It is with great pride and enthusiasm that I welcome you to GD Goenka School, Aurangabad. Our institution is dedicated to nurturing the minds and hearts of our students, empowering them to become lifelong learners and responsible global citizens.

At GD Goenka, we believe in fostering an environment that celebrates academic excellence, creativity, and ethical values. Our experienced faculty is committed to providing a transformative educational experience that prepares students for the challenges of the 21st century.

We strive to instill in our students a love for learning, a spirit of inquiry, and a deep sense of compassion. Together, we embark on a journey of growth, discovery, and personal development, shaping the leaders of tomorrow.

Library

Information is the heartbeat of meaningful learning in schools. Exponential growth, availability and access to information, particularly electronic information on the Internet does not imply learning. At the G.D. Goenka Library, we strive to create a learning environment that empowers students to connect with, engage with, and utilize this information in meaningful and purposeful ways. Fundamental to this learning environment is the development of a school-wide information service where learners have opportunities to access multiple sources and forms of information and the appropriate technologies to do so.

We have developed strong library and information services which are essential to a learning programme that values the cognitive, cultural, social, affective, technological, and spiritual development of students in a globalized, multicultural, and uncertain world. Librarians are actively engaged in the development of curriculum across all learning areas to ensure the integration of information skills, and a carefully planned library programme that is implemented collectively and collaboratively.

Computer Center

Information technology has become integral to every walk of life. At GDGGS, the state-of-the-art, fully equipped computer labs give access and a window to every kind of holistic learning. Qualified Teachers guide and facilitate usage by students for meaningful engagement and outcomes.

  • Facilitate technical support and assistance in conducting classes
  • Conduct training programmes in software application for faculty, students, and staff
  • Providing internet facility to all departments and hostels
  • Maintaining the school website

SMART CLASSROOM

Technology is an integral part of today's environment. We have been successful in bringing technology to our classrooms with the introduction of interactive Whiteboards or Smart Boards. This helps students get a better understanding of concepts through audio-visual learning. It also gives the students exposure to a variety of resources, enabling them to become thinking children. The classroom transforms into an interactive forum for sharing experiences among students, thus helping in widening their analytical faculties.

ART ROOM

Exposure to art and craft activities adds color, joy, and beauty to the lives of the students, enhancing their creativity and self-expression. The visual arts are an expression of creativity and imagination. It is an outlet for individual thought, visualization, exploration of inner creativity, expression of imagination, and interpretation of ideas, events, and environment. The endeavor is to infuse a sense of aesthetic appreciation in each child, an ability to enjoy and appreciate beauty. The elaborate art studios with the finest faculty guide the children to achieve finesse through numerous techniques and the history of art.

The Visual Arts Department has a wide range of activities:

  • Fine Arts
  • Craft Work
  • Clay Modelling
  • Sculpture

MUSIC & DANCE STUDIO

This is the place where young musicians begin their journey to become composers and where the children unwind and learn the aesthetics of movement. Classical Dance (Indian & Western), Modern Dance (Indian & Western), Choreography, Direction, Vocal Music (Indian and Western), Instrumental Music (Indian and Western) are all interwoven into the fabric of our educational experience. G.D. Goenka students learn to choreograph and are made conversant with various Indian and Western dance forms like Indian Classical, Jazz, Hip Hop, Salsa, and these are some of the most eagerly awaited moments for self-expression. Lessons in Indian and Western music generate rich cross-cultural exposure and sensitize the students to this fine art.

The Department of Music and Dance is committed to excellence, innovation, learning in action, and entrepreneurship. Our mission is to bridge dance practice and scholarship and to foster interdisciplinary inquiry.
